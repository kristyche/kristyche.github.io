How do I work?

Design & development process?
I like starting off with creative briefings or a discussion about the scope of the job - a brainstorming session while sketching out possible methods of execution is my favourite. I also like having feedback sessions where I present a few different concepts to see which ones work better.
Time to complete projects?
It depends on the complexity and number of assets required, but I've worked with deadlines as short as one day! However, the optimal time-frame would be about 1-2 weeks per job.
What if clients don't know what they want?
It's part of a designer's job to be able to visualise and conceptualise abstract ideas, so usually I go based off the look and feel, as well as what I think will suit the intention of the project (which means straying from the brief sometimes).
